Our Story

A Journey of Excellence

Founded in 2015 by master roaster Elena Rodriguez, Artisan Roast began as a small neighborhood coffee shop with a simple mission: to bring the world's finest coffee directly to your cup.

We work directly with farmers across Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala, ensuring fair trade practices while sourcing the most exceptional beans. Each batch is roasted with precision and care, honoring both tradition and innovation.
